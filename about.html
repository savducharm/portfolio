About

My Story

I am a UX/UI designer based in San Clemente, CA. Born and raised in Southern California, I developed my love for people, culture, and art at a young age as I bounced between art galleries in Laguna Beach dragging my eccentric grandmother in toe.

Driven by an authentic, albeit clich√©, calling to help people I found myself graduating UCSD with a degree in Sociocultural Anthropology and another in Global Health. Shortly thereafter I began working for an international nonprofit as a graphic and web designer where I was able to use my creativity for good. During my time there, I began to learn about and implement a human-centered approach in my work which sparked my interest for UX.

I am now moving into UX/UI design as it is the perfect way for me to integrate my passion for design with my compassion for people.
